Operational cadence management for the WXV Technologies Research & Governance Lab. This project holds the recurring routines that drive the weekly and daily rhythm of the research governance pipeline.

## Routines

- **Weekly Trading Plan** — Sunday 8 PM SGT. Planning precedes execution.
- **Asia Confirmation Session** — Weekdays 10 AM SGT. Daily session gate check.
- **Trade Journal Entry** — Weekdays 10 PM SGT. Mandatory end-of-session record.
- **Weekly Review** — Friday 6 PM SGT. Cycle close and lessons learned.
