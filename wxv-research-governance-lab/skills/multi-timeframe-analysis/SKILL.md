Perform top-down multi-timeframe analysis following the MTA hierarchy:

1. **MN (Monthly)** — Market regime and long-term structural context
2. **W1 (Weekly)** — Intermediate trend and key weekly levels
3. **D1 (Daily)** — Decision gate focus. This is the primary directional pivot level.
4. **H4 (4-Hour)** — Mandatory body close confirmation before intraday direction
5. **H1 (1-Hour)** — Entry refinement and session-level structure

## Rules

- **Top-down is mandatory.** Never start analysis from H1 upward.
- **D1 is the decision gate.** If D1 is ambiguous, do not force a directional call.
- **H4 body close mandatory.** No H1 entry signal is valid without H4 body close confirmation.
- **Trend priority rule.** Higher timeframe bias overrides lower timeframe signals on conflict.
- **No-trade zone policy.** When conditions are unclear or conflicting across timeframes, the correct output is "no trade."
- **SMC is a precision enhancer, not a replacement.** Smart Money Concepts overlay enriches but does not override the core structural analysis.

## Output Format

For each timeframe, provide:
- Bias (bullish / bearish / neutral)
- Key levels (support, resistance, decision points)
- Structure assessment (trending, ranging, transitional)
- Confidence level (normalized score)
