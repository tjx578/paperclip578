Produce the weekly master plan that governs research focus, pair priority, and session allocation.

## Weekly Plan Structure

1. **Macro Context** — Key events, central bank calendar, data releases
2. **Regime Classification** — Risk-on / risk-off / transitional
3. **Pair Priority Matrix** — Ranked pairs with rationale
4. **Session Allocation** — Asia / London / NY focus areas
5. **Risk Budget** — Weekly allocation across pairs and sessions
6. **Carry-Forward** — Unresolved items from prior week

## Responsibilities

- HYBRID produces the weekly master plan (Sunday/Monday)
- V6.0 provides macro regime input
- V7.0 provides pair priority and flow context
- V8.0 reviews and validates the consolidated plan
- WOLF validates risk budget allocation

## Rules

- Weekly plan must be published before Asia open Monday
- Mid-week revisions require HYBRID approval
- Unplanned pairs are not tradeable without emergency override from ULTRA
- Plan adherence is audited by PROTOKOL
