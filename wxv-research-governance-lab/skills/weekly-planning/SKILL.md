Produce the weekly trading plan following the WEEKLY_TRADING_PLAN_v3.2 framework.

## Rules

- **Planning precedes execution.** No thesis enters the pipeline without an approved weekly plan.
- **Mandatory weekly plan** at the start of every cycle.
- **Mandatory weekly review** at the end of every cycle.

## Plan Contents

1. Market regime assessment (MN/W1 context from baseline analysis)
2. Directional bias for the week (from ULTRA's top-down view)
3. Key levels and decision points for D1
4. Session window priorities (Asia/London/NY)
5. Instruments in scope
6. Risk parameters for the week (max exposure, per-session limits)
7. No-trade conditions (what would cause the week to be a no-trade week)
8. Review of previous week's journal outcomes

## Approval Chain

1. ULTRA provides strategic direction
2. HYBRID drafts the plan
3. WOLF reviews for risk policy alignment
4. ULTRA approves
5. Plan is distributed to the research team
