# Weekly Trading Plan

Produce the weekly trading plan following WEEKLY_TRADING_PLAN_v3.2 framework.

1. Review previous week's journal outcomes with MEMVAULT
2. Get MN/W1 structural context from V6.0 baseline analysis
3. Get strategic direction from ULTRA
4. Draft the weekly plan: instruments, bias, key levels, risk parameters, no-trade conditions
5. Submit to WOLF for risk policy review
6. Submit to ULTRA for final approval
7. Distribute approved plan to the research team

Planning precedes execution. No session runs without an approved weekly plan.
