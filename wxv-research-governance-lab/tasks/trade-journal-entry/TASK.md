# Trade Journal Entry

Record the mandatory end-of-session trade journal entry.

1. Collect all session outputs: analysis, scores, gate results, verdicts
2. Gather commentary from V8.0 (synthesis), WOLF (risk), QRCE (compliance)
3. Compile the journal entry in WOLF_JOURNAL_SYSTEM_v3.2 format
4. Validate entry completeness against the 9 constitutional gates
5. Append the entry to the immutable journal (no edits, no deletions)
6. Generate the board-ready approval packet
7. Submit to PROTOKOL for final governance sign-off
8. Once signed, the entry is sealed and timestamped

No journal entry may be modified after PROTOKOL sign-off. Any correction requires a new amendment entry referencing the original.
