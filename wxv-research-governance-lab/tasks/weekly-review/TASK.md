Conduct the mandatory weekly review.

1. Aggregate all journal entries from the week (MEMVAULT)
2. Review session outcomes against the weekly plan
3. Assess scoring accuracy and gate performance (WOLF)
4. Compile compliance summary (QRCE)
5. Produce the weekly review report
6. Submit to ULTRA for strategic review
7. Submit to PROTOKOL for governance sign-off
8. Record review in the immutable journal

Lessons learned feed into the next weekly planning cycle.
